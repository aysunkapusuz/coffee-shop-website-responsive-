Our Story

Cup of Tea has been built by two tea obsessed sisters. Starting out with just ten tea blends, Jane and Michelle have built up their offerings to over 25 sustainibly sourced and organic loose leaf tea profiles. Cup of Tea is a mini tea shop with variety of tea from East Asia to the US.
